Webpage 4


Colors

Something we haven't discussed yet, but is crucial for the User Experience (UX) of a website, is its simplicity.

  • Good websites are easy to use and don't overwhelm users with too many choices. Visitors usually have a specific goal in mind, so we should make it effortless for them. Web usability and testing are key concepts in UX. Reference: Steve Krug's "Don't Make Me Think".
  • Our goal is to make web browsers stay and keep the website captivating because they're just a click away from leaving.
  • The choice of text and background colors can make the webpage more vibrant and appealing to users.

For headings and paragraphs

<h1 style = "colors: colorname;"> ..... </h1>

or

<p style = "color: colorname;">..... </h1>

For background-color

<body style = "background-color: colorname;">..... </body>

or

<body style = "background-color: #Colorpicker hexvalue;">..... </body>

Here is the link where you can obtain the hexadecimal value for any color you choose: Colorpicker hexvalue

The syntax used to implement color to text and the background is not HTML, but CSS.
